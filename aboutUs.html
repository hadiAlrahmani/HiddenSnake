About Us

Welcome to HiddenSnake, a simple and fun twist on the classic Snake game! My goal was to create a nostalgic yet enjoyable game for everyone to play. As a beginner developer, I focused on keeping things straightforward while ensuring the game is both functional and entertaining. With its sleek design and easy controls, HiddenSnake is perfect for quick, fun entertainment and a thrilling challenge to see who can achieve the highest score. Thank you for playing, and hope you enjoy the game as much as I enjoyed creating it!
